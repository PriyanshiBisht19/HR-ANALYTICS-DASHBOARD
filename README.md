# HR-ANALYTICS-DASHBOARD

 HR Analytics Dashboard
 Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard provides insights into employee data, focusing on attrition trends, demographics, and salary distribution. It helps HR teams make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and workforce planning.



 Key Features
 
 Employee Overview – Total employee count, average age, salary, and years at company
 Attrition Analysis – Tracks employee attrition count and rate
 Education-wise Attrition – Distribution based on educational background
 Age Group Analysis – Attrition trends across different age groups
 Salary Insights – Attrition based on salary ranges
 Job Role Analysis – Attrition across different job roles
 Years at Company – Trends based on employee tenure
 Gender Distribution – Attrition comparison between male and female employees
